How It Works

Stablecoin Settlement & Tokenized Commodity Investment

Stablecoin Settlement
A stablecoin is a digital currency backed 1:1 by fiat currency or approved reserve assets. It holds stable value — unlike volatile cryptocurrencies. MOOZARS enables commodity trades to settle in seconds rather than the 2–5 days required by traditional correspondent banking. Full reserve transparency. Blockchain-verified. Counterparty risk eliminated at the point of settlement.
Tokenized Capital Flow
Tokenization converts ownership of a real-world asset into digital tokens on a blockchain — enabling fractional ownership and instant transfer. MOOZARS tokenizes commodity value at every stage of the supply chain: purchase, transport, refining, and storage. Accredited investors fund each stage collectively, creating new capital pathways into markets previously accessible only to established trading houses.
